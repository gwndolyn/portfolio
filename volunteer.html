VIA Project

I was involved in the planning, coordination and implementation with a team for our class VIA project at the Lions Befrienders Senior Activity Centre. We interacted and played games with the elderly to keep them entertained. We also realised the difficulties that the elderly faced and decided to assist them. In response to the needs of these elderly, we subsequently organized a school-based donation drive to collect daily necessities from the whole school before delivering it to the centre.
